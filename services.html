How It Works

Step 01

App & Assessment Unlock

Instant access to the Fitness Gurukul app. Complete a digital intake form — fitness level, lifestyle, medical history, and goals.

Step 02

Trainer Allocation

Based on your location and preferred slots, a dedicated personal trainer is assigned and connects via in-app chat within hours.

Step 03

Physical Assessment

The first in-person session is a comprehensive audit — baseline strength, posture, mobility limits. Ensures your routines are safe and effective.

Step 04

Macro & Workout Sync

Within 24 hours your 1-month digital blueprint is live. The app shows PT days, recovery days, custom routines, and Indian nutrition macro targets.

Step 05

Attendance Check-Ins

On training days, validate your session using in-app location tagging and selfie check-in. This ensures transparency and fierce accountability.

Step 06

Daily Tracking & Review

Log meals, water, and steps daily. Your coach monitors in real time. End of month, a full review of weight, measurements, and consistency sets up your next cycle.
