Our Story

From Vision to Community

How a single idea grew into Hyderabad's premier fitness community.

Fitness Gurukul was founded in 2013 with a simple belief — that everyone deserves access to quality fitness coaching that respects their body, their culture, and their lifestyle. What started as a small personal training initiative has grown into a thriving community of expert coaches who have helped over 40,000 people transform their health.

Our approach is rooted in Indian nutrition, science-backed training, and genuine care for every individual. We do not believe in crash diets or cookie-cutter programs. Every plan is personalized, every session is coached, and every milestone is celebrated.
