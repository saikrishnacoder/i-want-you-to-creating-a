Owner workflow

How to use the records after they appear.

This page stays separate from the client-facing site, but it still needs enough detail for daily use.

01

Review fresh leads

Start with the latest consultation requests and note the program, goal, phone, and preferred details.

02

Call or message

Use the submitted phone number to confirm timing, location, goal urgency, and whether the client needs doorstep service.

03

Assign the route

Match the person to personal training, nutrition, yoga, swimming, online coaching, corporate event, or community challenge.

04

Track check-ins and scans

Use check-in entries and AI scan notes to see mood, stamina, weight, and posture route before changing intensity or nutrition targets.

05

Use raw JSON

Open /api/admin-data when you need the same records in a raw format for copying into another system.

06

Keep client nav clean

This owner viewer is intentionally direct-only so visitors stay focused on programs, community, tools, and contact.

Data interpretation

Turn submissions into follow-up decisions.
